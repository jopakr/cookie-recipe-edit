This recipe is from my dad and they are a favorite among friends and family.

The secret ingredient is the coconut! Be warned, these will fly off of the plate!

History of cookies

Most people don't know the complicated past of the cookie. From the Finnish adaptation of the phrase, "cute as a hen house", the multitude of choclate chips represent the many chickens in a hen house, the cookie.

And when Napolean Boneparte' was starting one of his late campaigns norht into "Barbarian" country he came upon a "cookie".

Having had such a wonderful delicacy as he had never experienced before in his whole life, he proclaimed, "Leave these people be. For I have tried their 'cookie'"

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • White sugar
  • Brown sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Flour
  • Baking soda
  • Salt
  • Chocolate chips
  • Oatmeal
  • Coconut

Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 325.
  2. Beat the butter, sugar, eggs and vanilla together until creamy.
  3. Mix together the flour, baking soda and salt in a separate bowl.
  4. Add flour mixture to butter mixture slowly.
  5. Stir in chocolate chips, oatmeal and coconut.
  6. Bake for 10 minutes or until golden brown.
